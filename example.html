Lists Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium
  2. Gentlymix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moisr remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until serves)
